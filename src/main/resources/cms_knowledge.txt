CMS Computers (Computer Management System) Knowledge Base
=========================================================

Company Information:
- CMS stands for Computer Management System (officially CMS Computers Limited).
- Website: https://www.cms.co.in/
- Year of Foundation: Founded in 1976 by the late Ramesh Grover (Raju Ramesh Grover).
- Managing Director: Aarti Grover.
- CEO: Anil Menon.
- Headquarters: Bhandup (West), Mumbai, Maharashtra, India.
- Number of Employees: Over 4,000 employees.
- Presence: Operates across more than 100 locations in India.
- Main Business Divisions & Solutions:
  1. e-Governance Solutions: Services providing direct citizens access to government services (e.g. Bangalore One).
  2. Energy Management Solutions: Specialized automated metering and ABT/EMS projects for utilities and power stations (e.g. Neyveli Lignite, Lanco Anpara).
  3. Media & Broadcasting Solutions.
  4. Surveillance & Workforce Solutions.
  5. Transportation & Traffic Solutions: Automated toll management, traffic control systems.
  6. Systems Integration & Software Services.
  7. Cloud Solutions (AWS).

CMS Future Leaders (CFL) Program:
- CFL stands for CMS Future Leaders.
- It is a leadership program within CMS designed to nurture and train future leaders from new graduates, students, and young professionals.
- Key modules include mentorship, goal setting, technical & non-technical skill training, quarterly evaluations, and probation tracking.

CMS Digital Lounge Portal:
- Website: https://cdl.cms.co.in/
- This is the internal portal for CMS employees.
- Portals and sections include:
  1. Events@CMS: Sports, health, community drives (e.g., cycling drives in Mumbai/Bangalore), and tax savings.
  2. Watsup@CMS: Employee announcements, technical trainings, robust engineering and DevOps best practices.
  3. Employee Self Services: Profile edit, Employee search, Mediclaim enrollment, eLMS (Learning Management System), ePMS (Performance Management System), ERF (Employee Requisition Form).
  4. Engagement: Birthday wishes, opinion polls, quiz tests.
  5. Feedback/Support: We Care, IT Help Desk, and direct communication to the CEO ("Talk to CEO").
